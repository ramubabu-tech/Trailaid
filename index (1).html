Before anything else

Read me

TrailAid gives plain first-aid prompts for untrained bystanders. It is not a substitute for medical training or professional care. In any emergency, call 911 and follow the dispatcher — they can see help coming and will guide you. When in doubt, make the call.

Guidance reflects widely published bystander protocols (AHA Hands-Only CPR, "Stop the Bleed," Wilderness Medical Society heat-illness and snakebite guidance). No agency endorses this demo.
